More on my Comics Projects

In my undergraduate degree, I studied animation and narrative studies. It has always been my goal to one day combine all of my studies into an interactive comic, with animation, comic panels, and playful coding all together. Below, I share some more about my current and planned Webcomic projects, and a pitch for a future interactive comic on the next page.

It's been a long time since I've pursued animation fully, but what I've learned both from art and Library Science is how interconnected all these fields are, and how much you can learn about yourself and your passions by trying a little bit of everything.
