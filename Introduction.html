Hello and welcome to my art and library portfolio!

My name is Sarah Tillery and I am an artist and a librarian, primarily interested in comics! Here I will share both my library work, and my comics work, and show how they come together.

My primary work, until I started with the University of Arizona's School of Library and Information Science, was as a freelance cartoonit and comic artist. Below is my portfolio of work, including brief summaries of the two webcomics I worked on: Seeing Things and Recurrent Affections. There will also be a look into what work I've done in Library Science with the University of Arizona so far!


Comics

Seeing Things

When Aaliyah learns she has the ability to see otherworldly beings her life is turned around. But at least they’re willing to pay rent while she figures out what all this means.

Pencils and Inks by Sarah Tillery; Flats, Colors, Lettering by Ken Fergione; Written by Ken Fergione

Read on our main page or on webtoons! Currently on hiatus, but usually updates Thursdays.

Recurrent Affections

“Okay, okay, I’ll tell you what happened. It’s annoying, and idiotic, but… It’s my story. 10 years ago, I washed ashore. I made a life for myself as a tinkerer, an Ondyne lost on land. I met a Prince. I was stupid… I fell in love. Eight years ago, I left in the night. It was the right thing to do. We were… Both better off. I figured we’d never see each other again, you know how it goes. Well, you’d think I’d know how shitty my luck was by now.”

Pencils and Inks by Sarah Tillery; Lettering by Ken Fergione; Written by Sarah Tillery; Edited by Ken Fergione

Read on webtoon! Currently on indefinite hiatus.
